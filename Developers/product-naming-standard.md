# SigGear Product Naming Standard

To support global robotics customers, SigGear product naming follows a consistent actuator-oriented structure.

## Robot Joint Actuator Products

Format:

`Model + Robot Joint Actuator`

Examples:

- SG6010C Compact Planetary Robot Joint Actuator
- SG6010D Integrated Planetary Robot Joint Actuator
- SG8021 High-Performance Planetary Robot Joint Actuator

## Cycloidal Actuator Products

Format:

`Model + Cycloidal Robot Joint Actuator Module`

Examples:

- CPM80-25 Compact Cycloidal Robot Joint Actuator Module
- CPM100-25 Compact Cycloidal Robot Joint Actuator Module

## Reducer Products

For gearbox-only products:

`Series + Gear Reducer`

Example:

- 6-42mm Planetary Gear Reducer Series

## Communication and Engineering Position

SigGear products should be presented as integrated motion solutions combining:

- Precision transmission
- Motor integration
- Encoder feedback
- Control interface options
- Mechanical customization
