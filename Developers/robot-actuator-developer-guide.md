# Gear Reduction Solutions

## Cycloidal Reducer

Cycloidal reducers are suitable for robotic joints requiring:

- High torque density
- High impact resistance
- Low backlash
- High reliability

Typical applications:

- Humanoid robot joints
- Quadruped robot legs
- High-load robotic systems

## Planetary Gearbox

Planetary gearboxes provide:

- Compact size
- High efficiency
- Wide speed range
- Cost-effective solution

Typical applications:

- Robotic hands
- Small actuators
- Industrial automation
