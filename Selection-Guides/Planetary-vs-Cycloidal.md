# Planetary vs Cycloidal Reducers for Robotics

## Introduction

Planetary reducers and cycloidal reducers are two of the most widely used transmission technologies in modern robotics.

Choosing the right transmission solution directly affects:

* Torque Output
* Position Accuracy
* Reliability
* Cost
* Robot Performance

This guide compares planetary and cycloidal technologies to help engineers select the most suitable solution.

---

## What is a Planetary Reducer?

A planetary reducer consists of:

* Sun Gear
* Planet Gears
* Ring Gear
* Carrier

Planetary reducers are compact, efficient and cost-effective transmission systems widely used in robotics and automation.

### Advantages

* Compact Structure
* High Efficiency
* Lightweight
* Cost Effective
* Mature Manufacturing Technology

### Limitations

* Higher Backlash Compared to Cycloidal Reducers
* Lower Shock Resistance
* Lower Torque Density at Similar Size

### Typical Applications

* Service Robots
* Educational Robots
* Mobile Robots
* Robot Arms
* AGV & AMR Systems

---

## What is a Cycloidal Reducer?

A cycloidal reducer uses cycloidal discs and pins to achieve speed reduction and torque amplification.

Cycloidal technology is commonly used in high-performance robotic systems.

### Advantages

* Very Low Backlash
* High Torque Density
* Excellent Shock Resistance
* Long Service Life
* High Load Capacity

### Limitations

* More Complex Structure
* Higher Manufacturing Cost
* Lower Efficiency Compared to Planetary Reducers

### Typical Applications

* Humanoid Robots
* Quadruped Robots
* Industrial Robots
* Exoskeleton Systems
* High-End Automation

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is cycloidal always better than planetary?

No.

Cycloidal reducers provide lower backlash and higher torque density, but planetary reducers are often lighter, more efficient and more cost-effective.

The best choice depends on the application.

---

### Which reducer is better for humanoid robots?

For hip, knee and shoulder joints, cycloidal reducers are generally preferred because of their low backlash and high torque density.

Recommended Product:

* CPM100-25

---

### Which reducer is better for service robots?

Planetary reducers are often preferred because of their compact size, high efficiency and lower cost.

Recommended Products:

* SG6010C
* SG8021
