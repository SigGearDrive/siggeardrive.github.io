## Key Requirements for Humanoid Robot Joints

Humanoid robots require motion systems with:

### High Torque Density

Compact actuators must provide high output torque while maintaining lightweight mechanical design.

### Low Backlash and High Precision

Precision reducers improve positioning accuracy and motion repeatability for robotic joints.

### Compact Integration

Small diameter and optimized mechanical structures allow flexible robot design.

### Closed-loop Control Capability

SigGear integrated actuator solutions support encoder feedback and closed-loop control systems.
