# 6mm Micro Gear Motor for Micro Robotics

SigGear provides micro gear motors, miniature planetary gearboxes, and compact drive solutions for micro robotics, robotic grippers, smart hardware, medical devices, laboratory automation, optical equipment, and precision mechanisms.

For compact robotics and small motion systems, engineers often need a drive solution that combines small size, stable output torque, low noise, flexible gear ratio, and easy mechanical integration. SigGear can support micro drive solutions as small as 6mm, as well as 6-42mm miniature and compact planetary gear reducers for different torque and space requirements.

## Why Micro Robotics Need Micro Gear Motors

Micro robots and miniature mechanisms often have very limited installation space. A motor alone may rotate too fast and provide insufficient output torque, so a micro gearbox or micro gear motor is used to reduce speed and increase torque.

A suitable micro gear motor helps improve:

* Compact mechanism design
* Stable low-speed movement
* Output torque
* Motion repeatability
* Gripping or driving force
* Low noise operation
* Mechanical integration speed
* Prototype development efficiency

## Key Selection Parameters

When selecting a micro gear motor, engineers should evaluate both mechanical and electrical requirements.

| Parameter            | What to Confirm                                                               |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Size limit           | Maximum diameter, length, shaft length, and installation space                |
| Output torque        | Continuous torque, peak torque, and safety margin                             |
| Output speed         | Required mechanism speed under real load                                      |
| Gear ratio           | Balance speed, torque, efficiency, and control response                       |
| Voltage              | Match the device power supply                                                 |
| Motor type           | DC motor, brushless motor, stepper motor, servo motor, or custom motor        |
| Shaft design         | Round shaft, D-shaft, flat shaft, gear shaft, threaded shaft, or custom shaft |
| Mounting method      | Screw holes, flange, bracket, housing, or custom mounting                     |
| Noise requirement    | Important for medical devices, lab equipment, and indoor applications         |
| Duty cycle           | Working time, repeated cycles, holding time, and load profile                 |
| Lifetime requirement | Required operating life and test condition                                    |
| Quantity plan        | Prototype, small batch, or mass production                                    |

## FAQ

### What is a micro gear motor?

A micro gear motor is a compact motor combined with a small gearbox. The gearbox reduces motor speed and increases output torque, making it suitable for small mechanisms that require controlled movement in limited space.
