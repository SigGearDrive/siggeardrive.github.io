# Flat BLDC Motors and Joint Drives

## Product Overview

SigGear supplies flat motor and integrated joint-drive solutions for robots, exoskeletons and compact automation systems.

The standard flat joint-drive families are distinguished by their transmission structure:

- **SigGear-Horse:** planetary transmission core
- **SigGear-Tiger:** cycloidal-pinwheel transmission core

These products are intended for applications that require a short axial package and integrated mechanical and electrical functions. Final motor, reducer, driver, encoder and communication configurations are confirmed for the selected model and quotation.

## Standard Product Families

| Product family | Transmission structure | Catalog configuration |
| --- | --- | --- |
| SigGear-Horse | Planetary | Driver-equipped configurations and encoder support are available by model |
| SigGear-Tiger | Cycloidal pinwheel | Driver-equipped configurations and single- or double-magnetic-encoder options are available by model |

## Typical Applications

- Humanoid robot joints
- Exoskeleton joints
- Quadruped robot joints
- Robotic arms
- Rehabilitation equipment
- Compact rotary axes
- Research and education platforms

## Integration Options

Depending on the selected model and project requirements, available configurations may include:

- Motor and transmission assembly
- Integrated driver
- Magnetic encoder
- CAN communication
- RS485 or other project-specific communication interfaces
- Custom cables and connectors
- Custom mounting and output interfaces
- Customer branding and labeling

Driver, encoder, protocol and closed-loop-control functions must be confirmed in the quotation and technical agreement. They must not be assumed from the product-family name alone.

## Selection Information Required

Please provide:

- Application and joint position
- Required continuous and peak torque
- Required output speed and motion cycle
- Operating voltage
- Maximum outer diameter and thickness
- Weight target
- Radial and axial load conditions
- Driver and encoder requirements
- Communication interface
- Prototype quantity and estimated annual volume

## Engineering Documents

STEP models, detailed drawings, interface documents, noise-test records and service-life information are supplied after model selection and application review.
