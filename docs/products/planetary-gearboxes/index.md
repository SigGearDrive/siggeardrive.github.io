# Planetary Gearboxes

SigGear planetary gearboxes provide compact transmission solutions for robotics, automation, medical devices and precision motion systems.

## Published Series

### 6–42 mm Planetary Gear Reducer Series

The miniature and compact series covers:

- 6P, 8P, 10P and 12P
- 14P, 16P, 20P and 22P
- 24P, 28P, 32P, 36P and 42P

[View the 6–42 mm planetary gear reducer series](6-42mm-planetary-gear-reducer.md)

## Integration and Customization

Depending on the selected model, SigGear can evaluate motor matching, custom ratios, shafts, mounting structures, encoders, brakes, cables and connectors.

Final torque, speed, ratio, backlash and dimensional data are confirmed from the approved specification for the selected diameter and stage configuration.
