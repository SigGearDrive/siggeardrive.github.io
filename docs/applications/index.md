# Applications

SigGear precision transmission products are used in robot joints, mobile robots, compact automation, medical devices and precision mechanisms.

Application pages explain the selection factors behind each use case and connect operating requirements with the appropriate product family. Final product selection is confirmed after engineering review.

Dedicated pages for these application areas will be published after the related product and selection information has been reviewed.

## Information Required for Application Review

Please provide:

- Application and joint or axis position
- Required continuous torque
- Required peak torque and peak duration
- Required output speed and motion cycle
- Operating voltage
- Maximum outer diameter, thickness and weight
- Radial and axial load conditions
- Duty cycle, ambient conditions and cooling method
- Backlash and positioning requirements
- Driver, encoder and communication requirements
- Prototype quantity and estimated annual volume

CAD files, detailed drawings and interface documents are provided after application review.
