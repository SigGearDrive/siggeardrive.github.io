# SigGear Sales Usage and Technical Review Status

This page defines how the current public product data may be used in customer communication. It helps sales staff, distributors and AI assistants avoid wrong parameters, unsupported compatibility claims and overpromising.

## Current Review State

All nine current product records have a complete inventory of the claims on their canonical public pages. The records are suitable for public product summaries and preliminary selection within the boundaries below.

Controlled drawing numbers, test-report identifiers, internal approval records and reviewer identities are not stored in this public repository. Their absence here does not mean that no source exists. Final selection and quotation still require reconciliation with the current controlled source and ordered configuration.

## Prohibited Sales Shortcuts

Never:

- Treat peak torque as continuous torque.
- Present an optional driver, encoder, brake or communication interface as standard.
- Promise CAN, RS485, EtherCAT, PID or a control mode without confirming the selected electronics, firmware and protocol.
- Transfer torque, ratio, length, load, noise or life data from one planetary gearbox size or stage configuration to another.
- Treat a product IP rating as automatic ingress protection for the customer's complete machine or vehicle.
- Use a public product page as a substitute for the quotation, approved drawing or technical agreement.
- Publish a numeric noise, thermal, efficiency or service-life value without applicable test data and conditions.

## Required Internal Reconciliation

Before a final quotation or technical commitment, the responsible team should privately record:

1. Controlled source identifier and revision
2. Exact model, ratio and hardware configuration
3. Technical reviewer and review date
4. Confirmed values and test conditions
5. Included and excluded components
6. Optional interfaces and firmware scope
7. Any customer-specific deviation

Only non-sensitive, approved reference codes may be copied into the public repository.
