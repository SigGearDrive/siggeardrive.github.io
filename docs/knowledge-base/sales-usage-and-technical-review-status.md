## Prohibited Sales Shortcuts

Never:

- Treat peak torque as continuous torque.
- Present optional electronics as standard without confirmation.
- Transfer specifications between different planetary gearbox sizes.
- Use a public product page as a substitute for quotation approval or controlled drawings.
