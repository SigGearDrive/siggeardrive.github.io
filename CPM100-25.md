# CPM100-25 Compact Cycloidal Robotic Joint Module Datasheet

![CPM100-25 Cycloidal Robotic Joint Module](assets/images/CPM100-25.png)

## Product Overview

CPM100-25 is a compact high-torque cycloidal robotic joint module designed for humanoid robots, robot arms, collaborative robots, research platforms, and compact high-load actuator systems.

The module is designed for applications that require high torque density, compact axial thickness, low backlash, shock-resistant transmission, and reliable robotic joint performance in limited installation space.

CPM100-25 can be evaluated for humanoid hip joints, knee joints, shoulder joints, robot arm joints, and other compact robotic mechanisms where a high-torque cycloidal drive solution is required.
