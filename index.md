## Featured Product: 6-42mm Planetary Gear Reducer

SigGear 6-42mm planetary gear reducers are designed for compact motion systems that require small size, stable output torque, low noise, flexible reduction ratios, and easy motor integration.

The series includes miniature and compact planetary gearbox options such as 6P, 8P, 10P, 12P, 14P, 16P, 20P, 22P, 24P, 28P, 32P, 36P, and 42P.

Typical applications include:

* Micro robotics
* Small robotic grippers
* Medical devices
* Laboratory automation
* Optical adjustment systems
* Precision instruments
* Smart hardware
* Compact actuators
* Servo drive modules
* Industrial automation mechanisms
