# Humanoid Robot Joint Solution

## Overview

Humanoid robots require compact, lightweight and high-torque joint modules capable of delivering smooth motion and precise force control.

The most critical joints include:

* Hip Joint
* Knee Joint
* Ankle Joint
* Shoulder Joint
* Elbow Joint

SigGear provides planetary and cycloidal robotic joint modules optimized for these applications.

---

## Design Challenges

Humanoid robot developers typically face the following challenges:

### High Torque Requirement

Hip and knee joints must support dynamic loads during:

* Walking
* Running
* Climbing
* Squatting

### Limited Installation Space

Joint modules must fit inside compact robotic structures.

### Low Backlash Requirement

Backlash directly affects:

* Motion Stability
* Position Accuracy
* Force Control

### Weight Reduction

Every gram matters in humanoid robot design.
