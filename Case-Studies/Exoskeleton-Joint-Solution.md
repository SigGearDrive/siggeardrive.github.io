# Exoskeleton Joint Solution

## Overview

Exoskeleton systems require lightweight, compact and highly efficient actuators to assist human movement while maintaining comfort and safety.

SigGear robotic joint modules provide a balance between torque density, weight and integration flexibility, making them suitable for rehabilitation, industrial assistance and wearable robotics.

---

## Design Challenges

### Lightweight Design

Excessive weight reduces user comfort and increases energy consumption.

### Smooth Motion

Human-machine interaction requires natural and responsive movement.

### Compact Size

Joint modules must fit within limited wearable space.

### Reliable Torque Output

The actuator must provide sufficient assistance without compromising safety.
