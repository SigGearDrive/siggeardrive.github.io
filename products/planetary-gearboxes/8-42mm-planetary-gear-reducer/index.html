    8–42 mm Planetary Gear Reducer Series¶

    Product Overview¶

    SigGear supplies miniature and compact planetary gear reducers with nominal outer diameters from 8 mm to 42 mm for robotics, medical devices, laboratory automation, precision instruments and compact industrial mechanisms.

    The series is intended for projects that require a compact transmission and flexible integration with a motor, output shaft, mounting interface or sensor system. Final ratio, torque, speed, dimensions and motor matching depend on the selected diameter and project configuration.

    Available Size Range¶

    Series Nominal outer diameter
    8P 8 mm
    10P 10 mm

    The 8P 8 mm, 10P 10 mm, 12P 12 mm, 14P 14 mm, 16P 16 mm, 20P 20 mm, 22P 22 mm, 24P 24 mm, 28P 28 mm and 32P 32 mm product pages include their currently published stage, ratio, torque, efficiency, load and dimensional data. Detailed values for the other sizes remain subject to their approved model sheets and are not inferred from these published model pages.

    Typical Applications¶

    • Micro robotics
    • Small robotic grippers
    • Miniature robotic arms
    • Medical devices
    • Laboratory automation equipment
    • Optical adjustment mechanisms
    • Precision instruments
    • Smart hardware
    • Compact actuators
    • Mini pumps and valve mechanisms
    • Inspection equipment
    • Servo and automation modules

    Motor Integration¶

    Depending on the selected gearbox and project requirements, SigGear can evaluate integration with:

    • Brushed DC motors
    • Brushless DC motors
    • Stepper motors
    • Servo motors
    • Customer-specified motor assemblies

    Motor compatibility must be confirmed according to input speed, input shaft, mounting interface, power, duty cycle and required output performance.

    Customization Options¶

    Project-specific options may include:

    • Reduction ratio
    • Output shaft shape and length
    • Input interface
    • Mounting flange
    • Housing and installation structure
    • Motor integration
    • Encoder or brake integration
    • Cable and connector configuration
    • Customer branding and labeling

    Customization availability depends on the selected diameter, order quantity and engineering review.

    Selection Information Required¶

    Please provide:

    • Application and installation position
    • Preferred gearbox diameter
    • Required reduction ratio
    • Required continuous and peak output torque
    • Required output speed
    • Motor type and motor parameters
    • Input and output shaft requirements
    • Mounting method
    • Backlash requirement
    • Duty cycle
    • Noise and service-life targets
    • Estimated prototype and annual quantity

    Drawings and Technical Files¶

    2D drawings, STEP models, detailed ratio tables and application-specific technical files are supplied after model selection and inquiry review.

    Published specifications must not be transferred from one diameter or stage configuration to another without confirmation.
