# CPM80-25 Compact Cycloidal Robotic Joint Module Datasheet

![CPM80-25 Compact Cycloidal Robotic Joint Module](images/cpm80-25-main.jpg)

## Product Overview

CPM80-25 is a compact cycloidal robotic joint module designed for robotic joints that require low backlash, high torque density, impact resistance, and compact installation.

The module can be configured for robotic joint development where engineers need a compact reducer and motor integration solution for humanoid robots, quadruped robots, robot arms, exoskeletons, service robots, research platforms, and compact automation systems.

CPM80-25 is suitable for prototype evaluation and custom robotic actuator development where compact structure, high peak torque, and fast integration are important.

## FAQ

### Is CPM80-25 suitable for compact humanoid or quadruped joints?

Yes. CPM80-25 is designed for compact robotic joint applications and can be evaluated for smaller humanoid joints, quadruped joints, robot arms, exoskeletons, and service robots.

### What is the difference between CPM80-25 and CPM100-25?

CPM80-25 is more compact with 80 mm outer diameter and 10 Nm rated torque. CPM100-25 is larger with 100 mm outer diameter and 25 Nm rated torque. The final choice depends on torque, speed, installation space, load condition, and safety margin.

### Can CPM80-25 include a driver?

Yes. CPM80-25 can be evaluated as a standard reducer + motor version or an integrated version with driver option, depending on the project requirement.

### Can SigGear customize the mechanical interface?

Yes. Custom shaft, flange, mounting interface, wiring, connector, motor, encoder, driver, and communication requirements can be evaluated.
