# CPM80-25 Compact Cycloidal Robotic Joint Module Datasheet

> Product image, 2D drawing, and CAD model are available on request. Contact SigGear for the latest mechanical drawing before prototype or production design.

## Product Overview

CPM80-25 is a compact cycloidal robotic joint module designed for robotic joints that require low backlash, high torque density, impact resistance, and compact installation.

The module can be configured for robotic joint development where engineers need a compact reducer and motor integration solution for humanoid robots, quadruped robots, robot arms, exoskeletons, service robots, research platforms, and compact automation systems.
