# SG8021 High-Performance Planetary Robotic Joint Module Datasheet

![SG8021 High-Performance Planetary Robotic Joint Module](images/sg8021-main.jpg)

## Product Overview

SG8021 is a high-performance planetary robotic joint actuator designed for humanoid robots, quadruped robots, robot arms, research platforms, and industrial robotic systems.

The module combines planetary gear transmission, high-torque BLDC motor, integrated driver, absolute encoder, and robotic communication interfaces. It is suitable for robotic systems that require compact structure, higher torque output than lightweight joint modules, integrated electronics, and fast prototype integration.

SG8021 can be evaluated for humanoid robot arms and legs, quadruped robot joints, compact robot arms, service robots, research robots, and custom robotic actuator systems.

## FAQ

### Is SG8021 suitable for humanoid or quadruped robot joints?

Yes. SG8021 can be evaluated for humanoid robots, quadruped robots, robot arms, and compact robotic actuator systems that require integrated motor, driver, encoder, and communication interface.

### What is the difference between SG6010C and SG8021?

SG6010C is lighter and faster, with 80 mm outer diameter, 6 Nm rated torque, 18 Nm peak torque, 310 rpm rated speed, and 377 g weight. SG8021 is larger and higher torque, with 100 mm outer diameter, 10 Nm rated torque, 30 Nm peak torque, 160 rpm rated speed, and 671 g weight.

### Does SG8021 support CAN communication?

Yes. SG8021 is listed with CAN bus, MIT protocol, ODrive protocol, and USB communication support. Final communication details should be confirmed according to the exact product configuration.

### Can SigGear customize SG8021?

Yes. Mechanical interface, communication protocol, wiring, connector, firmware, control configuration, and application-specific actuator requirements can be evaluated.
